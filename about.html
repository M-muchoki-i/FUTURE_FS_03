Our Story

16 years of baking happiness in Thika

Family Tradition Since 2010

Mama Wanjiku started baking from her small kitchen in 2010, serving hot mandazi to neighbors in Thika. What began as morning orders has grown into the most loved bakery along Thika Road.

We bake everything fresh daily using Mama's original recipes and the finest ingredients. No preservatives, no shortcuts—just pure Kenyan baking love.
